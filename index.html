📍
100-Mile Residency Rule
The recipient must reside at least 100 miles away from the participating resort hotel. This is a required condition of the reward and applies to all destinations including Cancún, Orlando, and the Bahamas.

Advance Booking Required
A minimum of 30 days advance notice is required to book travel dates. Requests submitted less than 30 days before the desired travel date cannot be accommodated.

This complimentary hotel stay may only be activated once per household per 12-month period. Additional stays within the same household within 12 months are not eligible.
